FAQ - How to Download & Extract Garba File

Follow these steps to download the Garba file:

  1. Click the Download File button on my website.
  2. You will be redirected to Google Drive.
  3. Click the Download icon in the top right.
  4. Wait for the file to finish downloading.

To extract the downloaded file, follow these steps:

  1. Download on Play Store & Install ZArchiver.
  2. Open ZArchiver and go to your Downloads folder.
  3. Find the Garba file and tap on it.
  4. Select Extract Here and wait for the extraction.
  5. Once done, your Garba files will be ready to use!
